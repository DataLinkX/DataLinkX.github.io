DataLinkX Group

Join Us

We are the DataLinkX group, aiming to link data to science.

Collaborators

Collaborations are welcome. We are glad to work with researchers from different areas who are interested in exploring the mechanisms behind data. If you have a data-analysis problem, please do not hesitate to email me at jianghj@zju.edu.cn.

Prospective Students

We are looking for students with a strong interest in interdisciplinary research who want to make an impact on the scientific community. If you have any questions, feel free to email me at jianghj@zju.edu.cn.

Undergraduates

We generally do not accept undergraduates as research assistants. However, if you have a very strong motivation, feel free to email me.

If you would like to work on your thesis with me, please get in touch as early as possible. In 2020, I supervised four theses, two of which scored above 90.
