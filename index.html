Hangjin Jiang is Assistant Professor at Center for Data Science, Zhejiang University (ZJU). Before joining in ZJU, He obtained his Ph.D in statistics in 2018 from Department of Statistics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. He mainly work on developing new statistical methods (with theoretical support) for analyzing data from different areas such as biology (Protein, Genetics, scRNA-seq, fMRI etc.), astronomy and medicine. His reserach interests includes statistical learning (Deep learning and Machine Learning), biostatistics (computational biology) and Bayesian data analysis (Modeling and Methodology).
