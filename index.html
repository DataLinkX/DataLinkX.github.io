I am a Assistant Professor of Statistics at Center for Data Science, Zhejiang University. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D in statistics (Advisor: Prof. Xiaodan Fan) in 2018 from Department of Statistics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, and M.Sc in Applied Mathematics in 2013 from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I mainly work on developing statistical (machine learning) methods for analyzing data from different areas such as biology, astronomy and medicine. Beside problem-driven researches, I am also interested in developing statistical methodologies related to Bayesian Inference, Deep Learning and High-dimensional Hypothesis Testing.
