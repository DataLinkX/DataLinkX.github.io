About

Background

Before joining Zhejiang University, I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics in 2018 from the Department of Statistics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaodan Fan, and my M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics in 2013 from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My group — DataLinkX — aims to link data to science. We build statistical and deep-learning methods for biological multi-omics and for multimodal data such as time-series, spatial and network data, integrating information across dimensions to reveal the key features driving complex phenotypes.
